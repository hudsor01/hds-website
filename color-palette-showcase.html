Usage Guidelines

  • Primary (Midnight Black): Main backgrounds, text, and authority elements
  • Secondary (Electric Cyan): Primary CTAs, highlights, and innovation elements
  • Accent (Neon Green): Success states, secondary CTAs, and growth indicators
  • Warning (Electric Orange): Energy states, notifications, and urgent actions
  • Purple: Premium features and special highlights
  • Glow Effects: Use sparingly on key metrics, CTAs, and emphasis text only
