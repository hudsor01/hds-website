- Auto-publish all AI-generated blog posts to Neon (no manual curation) — automated content pipeline

- TOOL_ROUTES constant used for all hrefs (no hardcoded strings)
- DOMPurify `typeof window` guard acceptable for trusted n8n pipeline content
